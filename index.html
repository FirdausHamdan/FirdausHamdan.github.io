FIRDAUS

Assalamualaikum and hi, my name is Muhammad Firdaus bin Hamdan. I am 21 by this 8th November. I hail from the beautiful city of Ipoh, nestled in Perak in Malaysia. However, my journey began in the historic town of Georgetown, Pulau Pinang, where I was born and spent my early years. Since my father's honourable retirement from his distinguished career as a soldier in 2002, we have made Ipoh our cherished home. Witnessing my father's dedication and sacrifice in the military has instilled in me a deep admiration for his unwavering commitment to serving our country.

I am in the final semester of my Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Kedah, where I have gained valuable knowledge and skills in the field. I am passionate about harnessing the power of technology to impact society positively. In particular, I am captivated by the art of photography, which allows me to capture moments and tell compelling stories through a lens. During my free time, you will often find me on the football field, playing the sport I love with my friends. The camaraderie and teamwork that football fosters resonate deeply with me, teaching me valuable life lessons and instilling a sense of discipline and determination.
